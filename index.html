Our Services

Penetration Testing

Full-Scale penetration testing that simulates real attacks malicious hackers may launch that can include reports, retesting, remediation guidance, and other services. These tests come with tiers to optimally suit your business's needs. Penetration testing is the backbone of ensuring your network is secure.

Phishing Campaign

Customizable phishing simulations including hand-crafted emails and phone calls. Phishing simulations provide valuable information on the activity of employees.

User Training

Live cybersecurity classes that are taught by a real cybersecurity professional. These classes aid employees in preventing cyber attacks at the source!

Threat Model Creation

Development of application-specific threat models using a reputable cybersecurity framework. These threat models aid developers in preventing threats as they add new features!

Security Policy Creation

Security policies that aid organizations ensure their employees are following proper cybersecurity procedures!

Code Review

Manual code reviews by a cybersecurity professional to identify possible vulnerabilities based on known patterns.

Disaster Recovery Planning

Development of tailored disaster recovery plans to ensure business continuity during security incidents.
